Hi, I'm Rebecca

A UX and Product Designer based in Vancouver, B.C., currently studying Interactive Arts & Technology (SIAT) at SFU. Growing up surrounded by a community of creatives, I learned to bring critical thinking into a creative space. Through being in SIAT, I grew a love for learning about human experience design, which is where I became more fond of the research process.

My work is driven by curiosity and the desire to explore more creative and critical ways to interact with the world around me. I believe design is rooted in everything we do, and interactions go far beyond a screen and into our everyday lives.
